Breakout Game Prototype

The rules for Breakout are simple:
1.) Use your mouse to guide the paddle and bounce the ball to hit the bricks.
2.) If you miss the ball then you lose a life. Losing all your lives is Game Over...
3.) Break all the bricks and you Win!

Red brick must be hit x2.
Green brick gives an extra life.
Yellow brick makes ball "invisible" until 3 more bricks are hit.
